How to find out what platform i have

Last updated: April 5 2026


Tutorial

  1. Open windows settings.
  2. Open Menu and click 'System'.
  3. Scroll down and click 'About'.
  4. Expand 'Device specifications'.
  5. Look at 'System Type'.
    • If it says 64-bit operating system, download 64-bit or 32-bit version.
    • If it says 32-bit operating system, download 32-bit version.
    • If it says arm-64 operating system, download arm-64 version.
